Artifact Narrative

Why This Artifact Was Selected

I selected the Thermostat Project because it gave me a practical foundation for demonstrating how software controls real-world behavior. The original project required the use of state-based logic and system-level thinking, which made it a meaningful artifact to enhance for the capstone.

Enhancing this project allowed me to show growth beyond the original assignment. I was able to focus on the quality of the design, the clarity of the logic, and the way data could be captured or organized to support evaluation of the thermostat's behavior. This makes the project a strong representation of my progress in the Computer Science program.

Enhancement Categories

Implemented Growth Areas

01

Software Design and Engineering

The enhanced version improves the organization and maintainability of the thermostat logic. By making responsibilities clearer and improving documentation, the project becomes easier to understand, evaluate, and extend.

02

Algorithms and Data Structures

The enhancement strengthens the logic used to process temperature values, target settings, and thermostat state changes. The work demonstrates algorithmic thinking by making the decision flow more intentional and easier to reason about.

03

Databases

The database-related enhancement introduces persistent data concepts by planning or implementing a way to store readings, configuration values, or event history. This creates a clearer connection between the embedded systems artifact and broader application data management skills.
